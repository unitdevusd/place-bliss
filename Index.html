Professionalism

In Stockton we have well-established medical system and facilities, and we understand how to tend to each individual under our care. Families from all over the globe look for care homes that provide appropriate medical services and caregiving needs to their loved ones. You can trust our APOB team to handle this.

Compassion

We empathize with the challenges families face, and treat each resident with love. A Place of Bliss listens to your needs and provides attentive care.

Professional Care Deployment

Patience

As the caregiving community we are, our APOB team prioritizes service. We patiently attend to every resident's needs, understanding the frustrations and setbacks that come with physical limitations. A Place of Bliss patiently reassures and professionally assists.

Peace of Mind

A Place of Bliss provides caregiving services to numerous families and medical institutions. We oversee for families, selecting caregivers carefully to provide the best service tailored to your needs. Choosing APOB ensures the utmost peace of mind.

Effective Communication with Tranquil Living

Efficiency

To facilitate timely and efficient communication between family members and our caregivers, families can use our scheduling service. Caregivers can then assist in coordinating the daily lives of residents with their families, ensuring that seniors get the best of both worlds.

Communication

We systematize the physiological measurements of each resident, such as blood sugar, blood pressure, heart rate, etc. We record this physiological measurement data in the backend in real-time and provide instant communication with family members, allowing you to stay informed about your elder's condition instantly and with no time lag, ensuring their safety and care needs are met.
